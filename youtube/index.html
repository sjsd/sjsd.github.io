On this page I present myself and my project. You are one of the few who have been given access to gain an insight into my project.

I am a 39 year old Norwegian family father. I speak and write fluent Norwegian and can speak and write English. In addition to the Nordic languages ​​Swedish and Danish.

I've been an active user of YouTube since its launch. I have been following trends on the internet and I blogged long before blogging was something everyone did. I've written private blogs, technology blogs, corporate blogs and I've deleted blogs.

At times, I've ranked top of Google's search results while in other times I've been hiding on the internet. Now I have a new project where I want to do the opposite of the trend in Scandinavia: I blog and present content on YouTube in my own native language: Norwegian.

I write and speak a dialect called bokmål. This is understood by everyone in Norway, but also by Swedes, Danes and most in Finland. That's why my blog and my YouTube channel have a hit in all of Scandinavia.

Without telling anyone about my channel on YouTube, I'm starting to get some followers. It is clear that the audience likes to have presented content in their own language rather than in English or a language that requires subtitling.

I have an informal and private approach when making videos. Something that helps me stand out from the crowd.

As you probably know, Scandinavians love to shop online. This gives us access to all the world's goods. When we in Norway do not have access to everything, we can now shop where we find the item we want. The selection is so much larger outside the country's borders. In 2018, Norwegians shopped more than NOK 144 billion. 3 out of 4 Norwegians buy their products on the internet. The biggest growth is from online stores like eBay and Amazon. But also AliExpress, Banggoods, Wish etc.

Creating content here on my private website and on YouTube is for me a hobby I practice in addition to my professional work. All hobbies cost money, but I've now come to a point where I need your help developing my YouTube channel.

I can help you present products and goods for the Scandinavian market. You can help me grow on YouTube by giving me samples that I can present.
